About Royal Spark

Igniting Innovation. Empowering Warfighters.

Royal Spark is the dedicated innovation cell serving RAF Mildenhall, acting as the launchpad for next-generation ideas across the 100th Air Refueling Wing, 352nd Special Operations Wing, 95th Reconnaissance Squadron, 488th Intelligence Squadron, and 727th Air Mobility Squadron. We empower Airmen to solve real-world problems through rapid prototyping, emerging technologies, and creative collaboration.

Our Mission

To drive mission-focused innovation that strengthens readiness, enhances lethality, and supports our Airmen through cutting-edge solutions, cross-functional partnerships, and grassroots creativity.

What We Do

  • Manage Squadron Innovation Funds (SIF)
  • Develop rapid prototypes in our 3D printing lab
  • Host design sprints, workshops, and pitch events
  • Forge partnerships with industry, academia, and allied forces
  • Support fielding of new tech, software, and workflows

Let's Build the Future – Together

Got an idea to improve the mission? Visit our lab, pitch your concept, or just stop by to collaborate. Innovation is a team sport—and everyone has a part to play.
